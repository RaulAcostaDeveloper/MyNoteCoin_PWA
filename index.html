Welcome

This web app is made to help you to have an advanced control for your money. Insert amounts to make instant calculus to help you to manage your cash.

Please Share This Web App...! Just Copy https://mynotecoin.com and share! So you can help others to improve them economy.

Guide

Cash

Input your actually cash to manage the amount of money that you will control in the app.

Total

Check out how much cash you have for the total. The total of cash is calculated with the cash plus the amount of money in credit cards.

Remaining

The remaining is how much money you actually have to spend for free. The remaining is calculated with the total of cash minus the total of the expenses that you write in expenses and spend on week.

Safe spend for today

This section is very important, because you can see how much cash you can spend for free today without guilty. It is calculated with the remaining and the amount of days that will pass until your next payment.

Sections

You can open each section to edit your list.

Total of each section

In each section you can see how much cash is moving there, so you can have a great control of your money.

Element in list

Each section has his own elements. You can Add, Edit and Delete each element.

Spend on week

In this section you can write how much money you will spend in each day, so you can have a great control for your money.

Expenses

In this section you can create a list of expenses, so you will remind what for and how much you have to apart for these expenses.

Debit cards

In this section you can create a list for your credit cards. So you will see and remind how much cash you have.

Debts

In this section you can create a list for your debts. These debts will not affect the cash.

Debtors

In this section you can create a list for debtors. That means that you will remind people who debt you money. These debts will not affect the cash.

Pendings

This section is for expenses that you will make in future. It works like a to do list or an expenses list, and will not affect your cash.

Payment day

In this section you can see how many days will take until your next payment day. The days remaining are used to calculate how much money you can use for free each day until you get pay, so you will never run out of money.

Is build thinking on payment day as each fortnight.
